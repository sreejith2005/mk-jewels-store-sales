## Product Facts Captured

- Gold purity: 24K is 99.9%, 22K is 91.6%, 18K is 75%.
- Tunch means gold purity by percentage or karat.
- Wastage covers manufacturing loss, craftsmanship, labour, and process costs.
- Trade billing adds wastage to tunch and calculates fine gold as `Weight x (Tunch + Wastage) / 100`.
- Making charges rise with gold price because wastage is percentage-based.
- MK Jewels scripts state jewellery is BIS HUID hallmarked and diamonds are certified.
- IGI certified natural diamond bangles include a QR-verifiable certificate with gold weight and diamond specifications.
- The certificate number is engraved on the bangle.
- Diamond buyback uses a standard 10% adjustment on assessed diamond value.
- In diamond buyback, gold is valued separately using today's live gold rate and net gold weight.
- Cash buyback requires invoice, authenticity certificate, PAN card, and bank details, with bank transfer within 7 working days after verification.
- Diamond valuation usually takes 24-48 hours.
- Old gold is purchased at MK Jewels' standard bullion-market buying rate.
- Non-gold elements are removed before old-gold weighing.
- Old-gold final value uses post-melting weight, assessed purity, and today's standard gold buying rate.
- Old-gold valuation applies a 1% standard refining charge.
- Old-gold value can be issued as credit note, exchanged against new jewellery excluding coins/bullion, or paid by bank transfer where applicable.
