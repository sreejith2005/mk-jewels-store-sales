## 🔷 Objective 

To convert existing walk-in or known customers into micro-influencers who bring their friends, relatives, or community members to Ra Ra Ru Lounge through personalized events, in-store experiences, or building-level mini-exhibitions. 

--- 

## 🔷 Structure of the Program 

## Role Responsibility Example 

Business Development CRM (BD-CRM) Build one-to-one relationships, identify influence circles (office, community, friend groups), and create opportunities for group visits or showcases. Ankita Todiwala from Kotak Bank → invited 3 colleagues for a private jewellery preview at Ra Ra Ru Lounge. Store Manager Approve and coordinate in-store logistics for mini-events (high tea setups, styling corner, photographers). Ensure smooth experience. --- 

🔷 Step-by-Step Flow 

## 1. Warm Observation (During Visit) 

CRM mingles casually once the customer is comfortable viewing jewellery. 

Compliment taste/style. 

Build rapport by showing genuine curiosity (“What line of work are you in?”). 

## 2. Discovery 

Ask subtle questions to identify her circle. 

“You have such a refined taste — do your colleagues share your love for jewellery?” 

“Do you all celebrate birthdays or milestones together at work or in your society?” 

## 3. Bridge Conversation 

Once comfort is built: 

> “We actually curate private previews for special clients like you — where you can invite 4– 5 close friends or colleagues for a high-tea jewellery experience. Would you like to host one?” 

## 4. Value Proposition 

Highlight exclusivity: “Your circle gets to experience one-of-a-kind curation before it’s available publicly.” 

Highlight benefit: “You’ll also receive a special hostess gift or voucher for every friend who buys.” 

## 5. Action Conversion 

Collect details of potential invitees (names / phone numbers). 

Fix date/time for preview or building exhibition. 

Add entry in Ra Ra Ru CRM Sheet (Host Name, Circle Type, Date Planned, Expected Guests, Result). 

## 6. Event Execution 

Arrange High Tea / Breakfast / Lunch with light music and styling station. 

Mention Host Name prominently (“Ra Ra Ru hosted by Ankita Todiwala & Friends”). 

## 7. Post-Event Follow-up 

Thank host with message and small personalized gift. 

Send appreciation story on WhatsApp (“Ankita’s Circle made the Ra Ra Ru Lounge sparkle today 🔷”). 

Invite next circle from same network.
