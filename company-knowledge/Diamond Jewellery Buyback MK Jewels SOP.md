## **MK Jewels SOP** 

## **Diamond Jewellery Buyback & Exchange** 

# **Version 1.0 | Customer Experience SOP** 

## **OBJECTIVE** 

To ensure every customer receives a **transparent, professional and confidence-building explanation** of how the buyback or exchange value of their diamond jewellery is calculated. 

The customer should leave believing: 

**"MK Jewels values every component of my jewellery fairly and transparently."** 

## **STEP 1** 

## **Welcome the Customer** 

Smile. 

Receive the jewellery carefully. 

Place it on a clean white jewellery pad. 

Never start discussing deductions immediately. 

Say: 

**"Certainly Ma'am. I'll explain exactly how the valuation works. At MK Jewels we calculate every component separately so that you receive a fair value."** 

## **STEP 2** 

## **Verify the Jewellery** 

Check 

- ✅ Original Invoice 

- ✅ Authenticity Certificate 

If unavailable 

Inform the customer politely that valuation can still be done, but documentation will be required before payment.

## **STEP 8** 

## **If Customer Questions the 10% Deduction** 

Never say 

✅ 

"We deduct 10%." 

Instead say 

**"Ma'am, the gold portion is always valued separately at today's market value. Only the diamond portion has a standard 10% buyback adjustment, which is our transparent and uniform policy for all customers."** 

## **STEP 9** 

## **If Customer Says** 

"But I purchased it for 50,000 per carat."₹ 

Reply 

**"Absolutely Ma'am. At the time of purchase the diamonds were valued at ₹50,000 per carat in FG, VVS-VS quality. During buyback, the current market value remains 50,000 per carat, and only the standard 10% ₹ buyback adjustment is applied. Therefore, the buyback value becomes ₹45,000 per carat."** 

## **STEP 10** 

## **Documents Required for Cash Buyback** 

Collect 

- ✅ Original Invoice 

- ✅ Authenticity Certificate 

✅ PAN Card 

- ✅ Bank Details 

Explain 

**"Once the documents are verified, the payment will be transferred directly to your bank account within 7 working days."** 

## **Communication Rules** 

## **Always Say** 

- ✔ "Current Market Value" 

- ✔ "Net Gold Weight" 

- ✔ "Transparent Calculation" 

- ✔ "Every Component is Valued Separately" 

- ✔ "Fair Valuation" 

- ✔ "Today's Live Gold Rate" 

## **Never Say** 

- ✘ Loss 

- ✘ Depreciation 

- ✘ Your jewellery has become cheaper 

- ✘ We only give this much 

- ✘ We cut 10% 

## **Customer Psychology** 

Always explain in this order: 

1. **Jewellery Breakdown** 

2. **Diamond Quality** 

3. **Current Market Value** 

4. **Gold Value** 

5. **Diamond Buyback Policy** 

6. **Final Total Value** 

7. **Documents & Payment Timeline** 

This sequence is important because customers first understand **what is being valued** , then **how it is being valued** , and only then **why a buyback adjustment applies** . It builds trust, reduces objections, and reinforces MK Jewels' commitment to transparency and fairness.
