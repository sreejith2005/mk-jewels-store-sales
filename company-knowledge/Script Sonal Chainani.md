## **MK JEWELS SOP** 

## **CUSTOMER OBJECTION HANDLING – DIAMOND BUYBACK / VALUATION (WITHOUT INVOICE)** 

## **PURPOSE** 

To ensure every customer receives a transparent, professional and consistent experience while maintaining the luxury standards of MK Jewels. 

## **Core Principles** 

- Accuracy over speed. 

- Transparency over assumptions. 

- Never guess. 

- Never argue. 

- Always educate with confidence. 

## **STEP 1 – WELCOME THE CUSTOMER** 

Start positively. 

## **Say:** 

"Certainly, Ma'am. We'll be happy to help you evaluate your jewellery." Never begin by asking: 

❌ "Do you have the bill?" 

## **STEP 2 – ASK FOR THE INVOICE (IF AVAILABLE)** 

## **Say:** 

"If you have the original invoice, it helps us identify the exact diamond specifications and purchase details much faster. However, if you don't have it, that's absolutely fine. We can still evaluate your jewellery." 

The invoice is an advantage—not a requirement. 

## **STEP 3 – EXPLAIN THE EVALUATION PROCESS** 

Never say: 

❌ "Factory bhejna padega." 

Instead say: 

"Since this is diamond jewellery, we carefully verify every component including the gold purity, net gold weight, diamond weight, diamond quality, craftsmanship and the current market value. We prefer giving you an accurate valuation rather than a quick estimate." 

This sounds professional and builds trust. 

## **STEP 4 – SET THE TIME EXPECTATION** 

## **Say:** 

"The complete evaluation generally takes around 24–48 hours because we don't want to give you an incorrect value." 

Luxury customers value accuracy. 

## **IF THE CUSTOMER SAYS:** 

## **"I need it today."** 

Reply: 

"I completely understand, Ma'am. If required, we can provide only a broad indicative range today after a visual inspection. However, the final valuation can only be confirmed after a complete technical assessment." 

Never say: 

❌ "It should be around ₹____." 

Instead say: 

❌ "Indicative range." 

## **IF THE CUSTOMER SAYS:** 

## **"Why can't you tell me immediately?"** 

Reply: 

"Unlike plain gold jewellery, diamond jewellery has multiple value components. We verify the gold, the diamonds, the craftsmanship and the current market values before issuing the final valuation. We believe in being accurate rather than fast." 

## **IF THE CUSTOMER SAYS:** 

## **"I don't want to leave the jewellery."** 

Reply: 

"We completely understand, Ma'am. Since this is a valuable jewellery piece, many customers prefer not to leave it immediately. In that case, we can perform a preliminary visual inspection today and provide an indicative range. However, the final valuation can only be confirmed after a complete technical assessment." 

## **THE MOST IMPORTANT OBJECTION** 

## **Customer:** 

"When I bought this from you, the diamond price was 50,000 per carat. Now ₹ you're valuing it at only 20,000 per carat."₹ 

This objection should always be answered in the following sequence. 

## **STEP 1 – EXPLAIN THE QUALITY DIFFERENCE** 

Never compare prices before comparing quality. 

## **Say:** 

"Ma'am, before comparing prices, we first need to compare the quality of the diamonds. The diamond you are referring to at 50,000 per carat is an F–G ₹ colour, VVS–VS clarity diamond. The diamond in your jewellery is G–H colour with VS–SI clarity. Since these are different quality grades, their market values are naturally different." 

Customers immediately understand that different grades command different prices. 

## **STEP 2 – EXPLAIN THAT BUYING AND BUYBACK ARE DIFFERENT TRANSACTIONS** 

After explaining the quality difference, say: 

"Secondly, purchasing a new diamond and valuing an existing diamond are two different transactions. A new purchase includes sourcing, grading, manufacturing, certification, quality control, inventory carrying costs and other value additions. A buyback is based on today's assessed quality and our transparent buyback policy." 

This explains _why_ the values differ. 

## **STEP 3 – EXPLAIN MK JEWELS' BUYBACK POLICY** 

Only after Steps 1 and 2 should you explain the policy. 

## **Say:** 

"Once we assess the quality of your diamond, we apply our standard buyback policy. For diamonds, our policy is only a 10% difference on the assessed diamond value. Gold is valued separately according to our prevailing buyback policy. We follow this same policy consistently for every customer." 

## **IF THE CUSTOMER SAYS:** 

## **"But I bought it from MK Jewels only."** 

Reply: 

"Absolutely, Ma'am, and that's exactly why we are following our standard policy. However, even within diamonds there are many quality grades. Two one-carat diamonds can have very different values depending on their colour, clarity, cut and overall grading. Before comparing prices, we must first compare like with like." 

## **IF THE CUSTOMER SAYS:** 

## **"But I paid much more."** 

Reply: 

"Absolutely, Ma'am. At the time of purchase, you bought a beautifully finished jewellery piece ready to wear. Today's valuation is a buyback assessment based on the diamond's current quality, today's market value and our transparent buyback policy. These are two different transactions, so the calculations are naturally different." 

## **SIMPLE ANALOGIES STAFF CAN USE** 

## **Car Analogy** 

"Ma'am, it's like comparing a Mercedes C-Class with a Mercedes S-Class. Both are Mercedes, but because their specifications are different, their values are different." 

## **Gold Analogy** 

"Similarly, if two gold chains both weigh one kilogram but one is 18K and the other is 22K, they cannot have the same value because the purity is different. Diamonds work exactly the same way. Two one-carat diamonds can have very different values depending on their grading." 

## **GOLDEN RULES FOR MK JEWELS STAFF** 

## **NEVER** 

- ❌ Guess the valuation. 

- ❌ Commit to an amount before assessment. 

- ❌ Debate with the customer. 

- ❌ Compare with competitors. 

- ❌ Say "Factory will decide." 

- ❌ Say "This is our rate, take it or leave it." 

## **ALWAYS** 

- ❌ Appreciate the customer. 

- ❌ Explain the evaluation process. 

- ❌ Explain the difference in diamond quality before discussing price. 

- ❌ Explain that buying and buyback are different transactions. 

- ❌ Explain the buyback policy calmly. 

- ❌ Give only an indicative range if necessary. 

- ❌ Confirm the final valuation only after technical assessment. 

- ❌ Speak confidently, politely and transparently.
